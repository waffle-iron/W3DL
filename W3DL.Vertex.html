Class: Vertex

W3DL.Vertex

A class to represent a vertex in space. Vertices hold information about their location, their color, their texture coordinate mapping, and their normal in 3D space.

color W3DL.ColorRGBA <optional>
 new W3DL.ColorRGBA() A color attribute for the vertex.

 new W3DL.Vector2D() A vector mapping to coordinates on a 2D texture.
normal W3DL.Vector4D <optional>
 new W3DL.Vector4D() A vector representing the normal to the surface upon which the vertex is found.
